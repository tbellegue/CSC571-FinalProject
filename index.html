This interactive dashboard visualizes taxi trips in a region of Spain. It includes: a map with a heatmap of pickup and drop-off density; a scatterplot of trip duration vs. speed; a bar chart of the top 20 taxis by trip count; a line chart of trips by hour; a histogram of trip durations; all charts are interconnected and respond to filters at the top. A time slider lets you explore data by hour or play through the day (note: no date distinction). You can show/hide individual charts or all at once. A "Clear Filters" button resets everything to default.
