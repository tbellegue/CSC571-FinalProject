The map represents taxi trips in a region of Spain. There are four additional charts as you scroll down the page. All charts are interactive with each other and the filters at the top. A scatterplot that shows the distribution of trip duration vs. speed, a bar chart that shows the top 20 taxis by trip count, a line chart that shows trips by hour of day and a histogram that shows the distribution of trip duration. At the top of the page there is a clear filters button to reset each chart to its default state. There is a time slider that you can control and look at each hour, or you can press play and watch the data points change through out each hour of a day (note: this slider by hour by only, it does not distinguish date). There is a heatmap that will show the density of pick up and drop off areas. You have the ability to hide or show each chart or hide all or show all charts.
